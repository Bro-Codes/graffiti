About Me.

Basically I am an Emarati Abstract Graffiti artist, I do one of a kind drawings and big mural graffiti. Being born and raised in a creative media house was an inspiration for me, which fuels my creativity, and makes me stand out of the crowd and be different. All my drawings are done based on the moment and mental stage at that time non of the paintings are planned for “ go with flow of that painting moment “
